Motorbike FAQs

How do I maintain my motorbike?

Regular maintenance includes checking the oil level, ensuring the tires are inflated properly, and keeping the chain lubricated.

What should I do if my motorbike won't start?

Check the battery, fuel level, and spark plugs. If everything seems fine, try cleaning the carburetor or consult a mechanic.

How often should I change the oil?

Typically, motorbike oil should be changed every 3,000 to 5,000 miles, depending on the manufacturer's recommendation.

Motorbike Troubleshooting

Issue: Engine won't start

Possible Causes: Dead battery, fuel blockage, spark plug issue.

Fixes: Recharge or replace the battery, check fuel flow, and inspect the spark plug for wear.

Issue: The bike is overheating

Possible Causes: Low coolant, faulty radiator fan, or engine oil problems.

Fixes: Check coolant levels, inspect the radiator fan, and ensure the engine oil is at the correct level.
